About Us

Uma Dye Chem was established at Unava, Gujarat, India in 1984. Today we enjoy an eminent position among manufacturers and suppliers of Oilfield chemicals and Food grade chemicals. Our products are primarily used in dye making and fertilizers and the product line that we supply is composed of Ammonium Bisulphite Solution, Ammonium Sulphite Solution, Oxygen Scavenger, etc. We offer chemicals that are accurately formulated to match the specific need.

Infrastructure / Manufacturing Facility

Space, equipment and experienced personnel all play their part in making our facility one of the best in its domain. The manufacturing unit has the capacity to deliver on bulk requirements with ease and this is one of the many re-assuring aspects of the infrastructure that we have.


Our laboratory has the machines and equipment that facilitate the manufacture of the most effective formulations that are accurate and effective. Hygienic conditions and knowledgeable staff also help add up to the perfect picture.

Our Quality Assurance

Uma Dye Chem follows inflexible quality control measures to ensure that all the chemicals we produce conform to international standards for quality, efficacy and safety. Our company's products are perfectly in sync with the needs of the purposes for which they are employed. Our experts analyse those requirements and base formulations on the actual need.


The quality process follows the entire range of activity from needs analysis, to procurement of raw materials, to manufacture and final testing. The process also allows for adjustments that improve the quality while remaining cost conscious. Each lot of chemicals are tested for:

  • Purity

  • Color

  • Solubility

Water Treatment

ABS is used as a reducing agent to remove chlorine and other disinfectants from water, making it suitable for various industrial processes and drinking purposes.

Food and Beverage Industry

It is used as a food preservative and antioxidant to prevent spoilage and maintain color stability in certain food products and beverages.

Food Preservation

Oxygen scavengers are used in food packaging to extend the shelf life of perishable products. They help prevent oxidation, which can lead to spoilage, rancidity, and color changes in food items.

Textile Industry

It is used in the textile industry for bleaching and dyeing processes.

Photography

ABS is used in the photographic industry as a developing agent.

Analytical Chemistry

It is used in certain analytical chemistry procedures.

Beverage Industry

Oxygen scavengers are used in the beverage industry to maintain the freshness and quality of beverages, particularly those susceptible to oxidation, such as beer and fruit juices.

Paper and Pulp Industry

ABS is employed in the bleaching process of pulp to remove lignin and other unwanted substances.

Industrial Processes

AS is utilized in certain industrial processes that require a reducing agent.

Pharmaceutical Industry

In pharmaceutical packaging, oxygen scavengers help protect drugs and medicines that are sensitive to oxygen degradation. They ensure the stability and potency of medications over their shelf life.

Corrosion Prevention

Oxygen scavengers are utilized in industrial applications, such as in the oil and gas industry, to prevent corrosion in pipelines and storage tanks caused by oxygen exposure.

Boiler Water Treatment

Oxygen scavengers are used in boiler water treatment to remove dissolved oxygen from the feedwater, reducing the risk of corrosion in the boiler system.
